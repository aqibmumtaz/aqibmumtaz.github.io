Research Focus

Anomaly detection has significant importance for the development of autonomous monitoring systems. A systematic research effort has been undertaken to develop autonomous surveillance systems, resulting in several significant contributions.

▶

A fast-learning Deep Multi-Net (DMN) model is proposed for detecting violent anomalous actions with superior accuracy.

▶

A novel deep learning architecture is introduced to learn spatiotemporal representations in videos.

▶

AnomalyNet, an end-to-end deep representation architecture, demonstrating superior generalization capabilities.

▶

An integrated anomaly detection system for class-specific anomaly detection and alert generation.
